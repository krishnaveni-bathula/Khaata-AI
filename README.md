# 🧾 Khaata AI

> AI-Powered Voice-Based Expense Tracker

Khaata AI is a smart expense tracking application that allows users to record their expenses simply by speaking. Instead of manually typing every transaction, users can use their voice, and the AI automatically converts speech into structured expense data.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Managing daily expenses is often neglected because existing expense trackers require users to manually enter details like amount, category, and description. This repetitive process is time-consuming, causing people to skip recording small transactions and lose track of their spending.

Khaata AI solves this problem by making expense tracking as simple as speaking naturally.

---

## 💡 Solution

Users simply say something like:

> "Spent ₹250 on groceries today."

The AI:

- 🎤 Converts speech to text
- 🧠 Understands the transaction
- 💰 Extracts the amount
- 🏷️ Detects the category
- 📅 Identifies the date
- 💾 Saves the expense automatically
